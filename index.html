What age groups do you teach?
▼
We teach students from age 5 to adults. Our curriculum is adapted to different age groups and skill levels, ensuring every student gets appropriate instruction for their stage of development.
How are classes conducted?
▼
All classes are conducted live online via video conferencing. Students get real-time interaction with their coach, can share their screen to analyze games, and participate in interactive exercises.
What is included in the free trial?
▼
The free trial includes a 45-minute live class with one of our coaches, a skill assessment, personalized feedback, and a recommended learning path. No payment required.
Do you prepare students for tournaments?
▼
Yes! Tournament preparation is a core part of our advanced programs. We cover FIDE-rated formats, time management, opening preparation, and the psychological aspects of competitive play.
Can parents sit in on classes?
▼
Absolutely. Parents are welcome to observe classes, especially for younger students. We also provide regular progress reports and have dedicated parent-coach check-ins.

About Us

The story behind C4Chess.

A global online academy built on the belief that chess is a powerful tool for developing minds — not just champions.

Education × Chess × Psychology.

C4Chess is a global online chess coaching academy dedicated to helping players of all ages and skill levels improve through structured training and expert guidance. Our mission is to make high-quality chess coaching accessible worldwide.

Chess is more than just a game. It develops critical thinking, concentration, problem-solving ability, and strategic decision-making. At C4Chess, our coaching programs are designed to build these skills step by step.

We offer structured programs for beginners, intermediate players, advanced tournament competitors, and adults looking to rediscover their passion for chess.

C4Chess is an evolving ecosystem — education × chess × psychology × technology. We don't ask where you come from. We care about where you want to go.

Curriculum

What you will learn.

1
Chess Fundamentals

Piece movements, board setup, basic rules, and the objective of the game.

2
Tactical Patterns

Forks, pins, skewers, discovered attacks, and checkmating patterns.

3
Opening Principles

Center control, piece development, king safety, and common opening systems.

4
Middlegame Strategy

Pawn structures, piece activity, planning, and positional understanding.

5
Endgame Technique

King and pawn endgames, rook endgames, and fundamental endgame principles.

6
Tournament Preparation

Time management, psychological preparation, game analysis, and competitive strategy.
